GRK Medi Fabs (G.V. Engineering Works) is a Vellore-based manufacturer of stainless steel hospital furniture and medical equipment. With over 40 years of experience supplying to leading hospitals including CMC Vellore, we combine traditional craftsmanship with precision fabrication to deliver equipment that meets the demands of clinical environments.
